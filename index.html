CORE SERVICES

Monitoring and Evaluation System Development

We work closely with organizations to develop customized monitoring and evaluation frameworks that align with their objectives and outcomes. Our frameworks ensure the collection of accurate and timely data to inform decision-making.

Data Collection and Analysis

Our team employs a range of data collection methods, including surveys, interviews, focus groups, and observation, to gather robust and accurate data. We employ advanced analytical techniques to derive actionable insights.

  • Comprehensive data collection services, including surveys, assessments, and interviews.
  • Advanced data analysis using modern tools and techniques.
  • Data visualization and reporting for actionable insights.

Project Management Support

At AUSAR Consultancy (K) Ltd, we understand that effective project management is essential for the successful execution of your initiatives while integrating monitoring and evaluation.

  • Technical assistance in project planning, implementation, and monitoring.
  • Integration of MERL into project management processes.

Impact Assessments, Research Studies and Evaluations

We conduct rigorous impact evaluations to assess the effectiveness and efficiency of programs, projects and interventions. Our evaluations utilize both quantitative and qualitative methods to provide comprehensive insights.

  • Rigorous impact assessments, research studies and program evaluations.

Capacity Building and Training

We offer customized training programs and workshops to build the capacity of organisations in monitoring and evaluation practices, research, data management, strategic planning, organizational development, and more.

  • Building organizational capacity for effective MERL practices.

Data Quality Assurance

Data quality assurance (DQA) is a critical process that ensures the accuracy, reliability, and consistency of data collected, processed, and stored within an organization. It encompasses systematic validation and verification procedures to maintain data integrity.

  • Ensuring data integrity and quality through systematic validation and verification.
  • Development of data quality assessment frameworks.
