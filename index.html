About Us

AUSAR Consultancy (K) Ltd is a dynamic and forward-thinking Monitoring Evaluation, Research and Learning (MERL) firm founded to support organizations, institutions and projects in maximizing their impact through effective monitoring, rigorous evaluation, and data-driven decision-making.

As a newly established firm, we are dedicated to delivering innovative and client-centric MERL solutions that empower our clients to excel in their missions.

Services

We offer a wide range of MERL services, including:

  • Data Collection and Analysis
  • Project Management Support
  • Impact Assessments, Research Studies and Evaluations
  • Capacity Building and Training
  • Data Quality Assurance
